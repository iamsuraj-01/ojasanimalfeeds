About Us

A Dairy Revolution in Uttarakhand

We are a collective of passionate women entrepreneurs from Uttarakhand, united by a common goal: to revolutionize the dairy sector in our region. Our initiative offers "urea-free" nutritious animal feed to dairy farmers and the dairy industry, promoting healthier livestock, enhancing milk production, and fostering sustainable agricultural practices.

Recognizing the challenges faced by dairy farmers, including fluctuating milk quality, livestock health issues, and the impact of conventional feed additives, we have developed an alternative that is both safe and effective. Our urea-free feed is designed to provide essential nutrients naturally, ensuring robust health and improved productivity for all kinds of dairy animals. We prioritize sustainable practices in our formulations, promoting animal welfare and environmental responsibility. By choosing our products, farmers can enhance the quality of their dairy output while reducing reliance on harmful additives. Experience the difference with our innovative feeding solutions tailored for thriving livestock.
